Menu entry mb

Determines body wave magnitude for teleseismic signals. It needs an amplitude measurement on one or more P waves before, by convention read from WWSSN-SP simulated traces. The steps to perform to come to a magnitude determination are:

  • Make a source location (e.g. using Locate Tele) or enter a known location into the analysis parameter box.
  • Apply the WWSSN-SP simulation filter.
  • Measure amplitudes of P waves (Ampl & Period auto Peak, Ampl & Period auto Zero)
  • Select the menu entry mb.

From all P amplitudes determined a magnitude will be computed. The formula applied is

mb = log10(ampl/period) + sigma(distance)
