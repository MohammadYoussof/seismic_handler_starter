Menu entry Particle Motion

Display particle motion diagrams (hodogram) in 3 projections. After selecting this this option a new graphic window pops up. The window which is selected by the right mouse button is displayed in the particle motion diagrams. It does not matter which component is selected, the three components (ZNE) are automatically searched and displayed. The projections shown are E-N, E-Z and N-Z. To figure out beginning and end, the traces turn from green colour at the beginning to red colour at the end. In each projection the direction of the main polarisation angle is computed and displayed.
